The Pharaohs' Golden Parade

The “Pharaohs’ Golden Parade” — a majestic procession through Cairo celebrating Egypt’s ancient rulers as their royal mummies were moved to a new museum home (2021).

The “Pharaohs’ Golden Parade” was a historic event in Cairo, Egypt, where twenty-two royal mummies—including those of Ramses II and Queen Hatshepsut— were transferred from the Egyptian Museum in Tahrir Square to their new home at the National Museum of Egyptian Civilization. The parade celebrated Egypt’s ancient heritage with golden chariots, music, and artistic performances honoring the legacy of its pharaohs.
